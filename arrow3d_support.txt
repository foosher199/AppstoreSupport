Colorful Arrow3D Puzzle is a relaxing yet strategic 3D puzzle game that is easy to pick up and fun to master.
In each level, you tap cubes to launch them in their arrow directions. The challenge is choosing the right order so each move opens space for the next one. As levels progress, layouts become more complex and the timer gets tighter, testing your observation, planning, and decision-making.

Features:
Tons of handcrafted levels with increasing difficulty
Fully 3D puzzle boards with smooth camera control
Helpful boosters like Shuffle and Bomb to break through tough stages
Countdown-based gameplay that adds excitement and pace
Clean visuals, satisfying effects, and responsive controls

Whether you want a quick brain break or a deeper puzzle challenge, Colorful Arrow3D Puzzle delivers rewarding gameplay in every session.
