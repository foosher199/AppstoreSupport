NumberPuzzleGame-Offline is a clean and relaxing 2048-style number puzzle you can play anytime, even without internet.
Swipe to move tiles, merge matching numbers, and keep your board alive as long as possible.
Easy to start, challenging to master.

Features
Classic 4x4 number merge gameplay
Smooth controls and quick sessions
Play fully offline
Undo, Shuffle, and Eliminate helper skills
Save progress automatically
Track best score and highest tile

How to Play
Swipe up, down, left, or right to move all tiles
Matching numbers merge into a larger number
New tiles appear after each move
No moves left = game over
