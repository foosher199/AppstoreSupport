WorkMark Camera — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026
Last Updated: July 14, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use WorkMark Camera (Chinese name: 工作水印相机; the “App”), an iOS watermark camera application that overlays time, location, project details, and other fields on photos you capture or import. The App’s bundle identifier is com.bigfish.WatermarkCamera.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Related terms are in our Terms of Use.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Library

With your permission, the App uses:

  • Camera — to preview and capture photos with watermarks
  • Photo Library (read) — to select existing photos for watermarking
  • Photo Library (write) — to save processed photos to your library

Photos are composited on your device. We do not upload your photos or videos to our servers.

Location Information

If you grant “While Using the App” location access, the App reads your current location to display an address or coordinates in watermarks (depending on the template and fields you enable). Location and reverse geocoding are handled by Apple system services. You may deny access; related watermark fields may be unavailable or show placeholders.

The App does not track your location in the background.

Subscription and Purchase Information

If you subscribe, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store (StoreKit). We do not receive your payment card details. The App reads subscription status on your device to unlock features (such as premium templates and higher export resolution), including:

  • Product identifier and whether a subscription is active
  • Plan type (weekly, monthly, or yearly)
  • Transaction and entitlement information returned by Apple (for validation and restore purchases)

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your iPhone:

  • Selected watermark templates and field settings
  • Custom watermark templates and edited content
  • Watermark position, project name, notes, and similar inputs
  • App preferences such as your last selected template

This data is kept on the device (for example, in UserDefaults) and is not uploaded to our servers. Uninstalling the App removes this local data.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Account registration data (the App does not require sign-in)
  • Photos, videos, or watermark files uploaded to our servers
  • Contacts, calendars, or other data unrelated to core features
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data
  • Analytics through third-party analytics SDKs

How We Use Information

We use the information above only to:

  • Capture, preview, and save watermarked photos
  • Display enabled fields such as time, location, and project details in watermarks
  • Save and restore your templates and editing preferences
  • Validate App Store subscriptions, restore purchases, and apply membership benefits
  • Respond to support requests

Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Where Data Is Stored

Your photos remain in your Photo Library under your control. Template and preference data stays on your device. Except for Apple App Store subscription validation, the App does not rely on our own cloud database to store your content.

How Long We Keep Data

  • Photos in your library are kept or deleted by you.
  • Template and editing data remains on the device until you uninstall the App.
  • Uninstalling the App deletes local preferences and custom templates.

Deleting Data

You can delete the App from iPhone Storage in Settings to clear local template and preference data. Photos already saved to your library must be deleted in the Photos app. Deleting the App does not cancel an App Store subscription; manage subscriptions in Apple ID settings.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information is involved only in these cases:

  • Apple — App Store subscriptions and system camera, photo library, and location permission handling, subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law or to protect users, our service, or the public.

Tracking

We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and we do not show targeted advertising in the App.

Security

The App processes photos locally by default. Subscription validation uses Apple’s official mechanisms. No app can guarantee perfect security; please keep your device secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will help address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
