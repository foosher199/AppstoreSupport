WorkStamp Camera is built for field work, project documentation, and everyday check-in workflows. Its goal is simple: make watermarked photo capture smoother, clearer, and easier to use.

Instead of trying to be an overloaded “all-in-one camera suite,” WorkStamp Camera focuses on what many users actually need:
live preview, rich watermark templates, flexible field editing, and one-tap save to the photo library.

Core Features
1. Capture photos with live watermark overlay
2. Import photos from the library and add watermarks
3. Multiple preset templates (engineering, attendance, travel, transport, and more)
4. Custom watermark templates and fields
5. Drag to reposition watermarks
6. Show time, location, coordinates, notes, and other fields (depending on template and permissions)
7. Save watermarked photos to the photo library
8. Membership unlocks more templates and higher export resolution

Why WorkStamp Camera
