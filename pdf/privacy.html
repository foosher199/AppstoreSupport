PDF Workshop — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026
Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use PDF Workshop (the “App”), a macOS application that converts PDF files to DOCX, XLSX, and PPTX. The App’s bundle identifier is com.bigfish.TransferPDFtoDocx.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Related terms are in our Terms of Use.

Information We Collect

Account Information (Sign in with Apple)

Conversion features require Sign in with Apple. Our service receives and stores:

  • Your Apple user identifier
  • An internal account ID we assign to your account
  • Your display name, if you choose to share it during sign-in
  • Your email address, if you choose to share it during sign-in (Apple may only provide this the first time you sign in)

We do not receive your Apple ID password. Sign-in is handled by Apple.

User Content (PDF Files)

When you start a conversion, the PDF you select is uploaded to our servers. We may store:

  • The PDF file while conversion is in progress
  • The converted output file until you download it or it is removed under our retention rules below
  • Task metadata such as file name, file size, page count, output format, and conversion status

We use your files only to provide the conversion service. We do not use them for advertising, and we do not use them to train machine-learning models.

Subscription and Purchase Information

If you subscribe, payment is processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details. We store subscription-related information needed to provide your plan, such as:

  • Product identifier and subscription status
  • Apple transaction identifiers (for example, original transaction ID)
  • Subscription start and expiration dates
  • Remaining usage quota for your plan

Information Stored on Your Mac

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Access token — in the macOS Keychain, used to keep you signed in
  • Download folder preference — a security-scoped bookmark for the folder you choose for saving converted files

The access token is sent to our servers with API requests to authenticate your account. Other local preferences are not uploaded.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendars, or photos (other than PDF files you choose to convert)
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data
  • Analytics through third-party analytics SDKs

How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate requests to our API
  • Convert PDF files and deliver results
  • Apply plan limits shown in the App (file size, page count, and usage quota)
  • Show conversion history in the App
  • Validate App Store subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Respond to support requests and address misuse of the service

Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Where Data Is Stored

Uploaded files and conversion results are stored in cloud object storage. Account, subscription, and task records are stored in our database. These systems are operated by third-party hosting providers we use to run the service.

How Long We Keep Data

  • Uploaded and converted files are removed from cloud storage within about 24 hours after a task is created, or sooner if you delete the task or your account.
  • After files are removed, task history entries may remain so the App can show past conversions (including when downloads are no longer available).
  • Account and subscription records are kept while your account remains active.

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account from Settings using the Delete Account link at the bottom of the page. This permanently deletes:

  • Your profile and sign-in records on our servers
  • Subscription records linked to your account on our servers
  • Conversion history and quota records
  • Any PDF or converted files still stored for your account

Deleting your account does not cancel an App Store subscription. Cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.

Signing out or deleting your account clears the session in the App. Local Keychain and folder preferences may also be cleared; uninstalling the App removes local data on your Mac.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and App Store subscription processing, subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Service providers — hosting, database, and file storage providers that help us operate the App and process conversions.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law or to protect users, our service, or the public.

Tracking

We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and we do not show targeted advertising in the App.

Security

Communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS. Access tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain. No online service can guarantee perfect security.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

International Users

Your information may be processed on servers outside your country. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred and processed in those locations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
