Overview:

BatchPicCompress is a local batch image compression app built for Mac—simple, fast, and privacy-focused. Drop your images into the window to start processing. Everything runs on your Mac; nothing is uploaded to the cloud.


Features:

1.Supports PNG and JPEG. Compression starts automatically after you add files.
2.Supports folder drag-and-drop. The app automatically finds all supported images in the folder.
3.Supports replacing the original file after compression.
4.Supports batch processing with clear status for each image: pending, processing, complete, or failed.
5.Supports side-by-side compare preview so you can check the original and compressed result before you use the files.
6.Local and private—no server uploads. Your images stay on your device.
7.Multiple appearance themes—choose the look you prefer.
